Lighting Setup

Basic Lighting Concepts

Learn the fundamental principles of lighting for video production.

Three-Point Lighting


1. Key Light
   - Main light source
   - Position: 45° from subject
   - Height: Slightly above eye level
   - Intensity: 100% (reference)

2. Fill Light
   - Softens shadows
   - Position: Opposite key light
   - Height: Eye level
   - Intensity: 50% of key light

3. Back Light
   - Creates separation
   - Position: Behind subject
   - Height: Above subject
   - Intensity: 75% of key light

Lighting Equipment

Understanding different types of lights and their applications.

Light Types and Modifiers


1. LED Panels
   - Daylight balanced (5600K)
   - Tungsten balanced (3200K)
   - Bi-color adjustable
   - Dimming capability

2. Softboxes
   - Diffused light
   - Even spread
   - Reduced shadows
   - Portrait friendly

3. Light Modifiers
   - Diffusion material
   - Barn doors
   - Grids
   - Color gels

4. Natural Light
   - Window light
   - Reflectors
   - Diffusion screens
   - Sun positioning

Lighting Techniques

Common Lighting Setups


1. Interview Lighting
   - Key light at 45°
   - Fill light at -45°
   - Hair light from behind
   - Background light optional

2. Product Lighting
   - Main light overhead
   - Fill cards below
   - Rim lights for edges
   - Clean white background

3. Scene Lighting
   - Motivated sources
   - Practical lights
   - Ambient fill
   - Shadow control

4. Green Screen
   - Even background lighting
   - Subject separation
   - No spill on subject
   - Proper exposure

Practice Exercise

Set up a basic interview lighting arrangement that includes:

  • Three-point lighting setup
  • Proper light modifiers
  • Background consideration
  • Color temperature matching

Interview Lighting Setup:

1. Key Light Setup
   - LED panel with softbox
   - 45° to subject's right
   - Height: Just above eye level
   - Distance: 4-6 feet from subject

2. Fill Light Setup
   - LED panel with diffusion
   - 45° to subject's left
   - Height: Eye level
   - Power: 50% of key light

3. Back Light Setup
   - Small LED with grid
   - Directly behind subject
   - Height: 2 feet above head
   - Power: 75% of key light

4. Additional Considerations
   - Check for eye lights
   - Control ambient light
   - Monitor for shadows
   - Test with camera
