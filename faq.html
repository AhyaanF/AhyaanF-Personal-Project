Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our course.

What prerequisites do I need for this course?

+

No prior experience is required! Our course starts from the basics and gradually progresses to advanced topics. Basic computer skills and enthusiasm to learn are all you need.

How long does it take to complete the course?

+

The course is self-paced, but typically takes 3-6 months to complete if studying 5-10 hours per week. You have lifetime access to all materials.
