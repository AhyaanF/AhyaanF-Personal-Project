Practice Exercise

Create an audio mix that demonstrates:

  • Clean dialog editing
  • Music and SFX integration
  • Proper level balance
  • Professional transitions

Example Audio Mix:

1. Dialog Track
   - Clean up noise
   - Apply EQ (HPF at 100Hz)
   - Light compression (2:1 ratio)
   - Level at -12dB average

2. Music Track
   - Fade in/out (3s)
   - Level at -18dB under dialog
   - EQ cut at dialog frequencies
   - Dynamic automation

3. Sound Effects
   - Spot effects at -15dB
   - Ambient bed at -24dB
   - Stereo placement
   - Impact enhancement

4. Final Mix
   - Dialog centered
   - Music stereo width
   - Effects panned
   - Master at -3dB peak
